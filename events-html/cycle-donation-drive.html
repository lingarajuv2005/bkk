Cycle Donation Drive

Date: November 25, 2025

Event Highlights

Thanks to our generous donors, we were able to provide bicycles to over 50 students, helping them commute to school more easily and attend classes regularly.

  • 50+ bicycles distributed to deserving students
  • Support from local sponsors and volunteers
  • Improved school attendance in the community
  • Awareness created about the importance of education

Photo Gallery

Impact

This initiative has inspired more support from the neighborhood, allowing children to reach their schools safely and quickly. We thank all donors whose kindness brought smiles to our students and their families.
