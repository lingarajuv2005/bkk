Children's Day Celebration

Date: November 14, 2025

Event Highlights

Our Children's Day Celebration brought together dozens of children, parents, and volunteers for an uplifting day filled with learning and joy. We organized creative arts sessions, engaging games, and inspiring talks from our educators.

  • Team-based games and fun activities
  • Art and craft workshops led by volunteers
  • Motivational storytelling by teachers
  • Special snacks and gifts for all children

Photo Gallery

Impact

Days like Children's Day strengthen our sense of community and build lasting memories for the children. We thank the volunteers and donors whose generosity made this event possible, helping nurture hope and happiness in every child.
